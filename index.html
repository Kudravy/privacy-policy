Privacy Policy

Odi Clo  ·  Last updated: April 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Odi Clo ("we", "us", or "our") handles information in connection with the Automation MCP application used to manage Pinterest content for our business.

1. Information We Access

Our application accesses the Pinterest API solely on behalf of the account owner (Odi Clo) to perform the following actions:

  • Read Pinterest board information
  • Publish pins on behalf of the account owner

No personal data of third parties is collected, stored, or processed.

2. How We Use Information

All API access is limited to internal business automation — specifically, automating the publishing of product images and descriptions to Pinterest boards owned by Odi Clo. We do not sell, share, or transfer any data to third parties.

3. Data Storage

This application does not maintain a database of user information. Access tokens are stored locally on the owner's device and are never transmitted to third-party services.

4. Third-Party Services

This application uses the Pinterest API. Use of Pinterest is subject to Pinterest's own Privacy Policy.
